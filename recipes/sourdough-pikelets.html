Sourdough pikelets

Overview

Sourdough pikelets (crumpets), made with sourdough discard. Inspired by King Arthur Baking Company's recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (227g) sourdough starter, unfed/discard
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/8 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients.
  2. Heat griddle or pan and oil or apply butter.
  3. Add mixture to pan in the size you'd like.
  4. Cook until you see bubbles on the top, then flip and cook for another minute or two.
  5. Serve.
