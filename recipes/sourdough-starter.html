Sourdough Starter

Overview

A sourdough starter is a culture containing flour, water, friendly bacteria like lactobacilli, and wild yeast. This elements act together to leaven (cause to rise) your bread and give it great flavor.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water in jar.
  2. Wait.
  3. Discard half of mixture, add equal parts flour and water.
  4. Do this until mixture doubles in size between "feedings"
