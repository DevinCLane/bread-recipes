Tartine Loaf

Overview

A country-style rustic sourdough loaf, inspired by Tartine in San Francisco.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Ripe sourdough starter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. 800g water in bowl, add 150g sourdough starter, mix together.
  2. Add 1000g of bread flour (optional: add your preferred percentage of whole wheat flour. Consider 10-30% to start). Mix until combined.
  3. Wait between 30mins-2hrs (or longer) for autolyze, then add 50g water and 20-25g salt. Mix well.
  4. Stretch and fold dough at 30 minute intervals for 3 hours (6 stretch and folds).
  5. Place in refrigerator overnight.
  6. Remove from refrigerator next day, divide loaf in two. Create initial shape by tugging loaf upon dry counter. Wait 20 mins.
  7. Create final shape by tugging loaf against counter. Let sit seam side down while you prepare proofing baskets or bowls: place clean tea towels inside bowl, coat with 50/50 rice flour and AP flour.
  8. Let proof for 1-3 hrs.
  9. Preheat oven to 500F with dutch oven inside for 1 hour.
  10. Using oven mitts, carefully remove dutch oven from oven. Transfer dough into dutch oven. Cover and return to oven. Bake 25 mins.
  11. Reduce heat to 450, bake 10 mins.
  12. Remove lid, bake until golden brown on top, approximately 10-20 minutes.
  13. Remove from dutch oven, and let cool at least 45 minutes before slicing and serving.
